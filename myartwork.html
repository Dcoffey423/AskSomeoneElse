My Artwork

Welcome to my artwork through out the years! Below, you can see my rapid progress. I hope you enjoy my Journey this far!

While there is not much artwork from this time, These are a few pieces I was able to find! Most of them are from my deviantart and my very early instagram post!

"Me (2015)"

I believe the best way to start this journey out is a self portrait! I drew myself often when I was younger, Most of my work had to do with me or a self insert (a original character).

For The Fox Productions (2015)

This piece was made for a YouTube Channel with my group of friends in middle school, While many of the videos have been deleted.

This one still remains!

This is when I first started using a tablet and working on shading, mostly using multiply and add layers. During this time I also focused on a lineless artstyle.

Some Random Redraw :0 (2018)

While finding artwork of my Original Character (OC) Christina, This was one of many I had found. Christina was made back in 2015, during my early stages of art, and to this day, I still find myself drawing her. She was heavily inspired from the 2014 indie horror game, Five Nights At Freddy's.

Socks! ||OC|| (2018)

This was a fun piece I made at the time to attempt to get rid of a artblock! Drawing animals has always helped me to get rid of my block.

A King and His Queen (2019)

This was a very experimental piece. Originally, I was planning on testing my limits, seeing how much I had improved through the year!

This year had to be the biggest year for my art progress. Due to quarantine. Ontop of this, this is the time I shifted alot of focus to working/creating a webcomic. Through character design to actual panels. I had filled entire sketchbooks full of art. At this point in time, this was my 'golden age' of my current work. The time I had the most improvement.

Christina {OLD OC} (2020)

This is the last drawing I had done in a lineless artstyle. As you can see Christina, An OC I had created back in 2015. Had a huge impact on my artwork!
